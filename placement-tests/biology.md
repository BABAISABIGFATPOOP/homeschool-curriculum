# Biology Placement Test

**Instructions:** Answer what you can. Stop when questions become too difficult.

## Section A (Grade 4–5 Level)

**1.** Name three things all living things need to survive.

**2.** Label the basic parts of a plant: What does each part do? (roots, stem, leaves, flower)

**3.** What is a **habitat**? Give an example.

**4.** Put these in order from smallest to largest: elephant, ant, dog, whale, cat

**5.** Name one animal from each group: mammal, reptile, bird, fish, amphibian

**6.** What is **photosynthesis** in simple terms?

**7.** What is the difference between a **producer** and a **consumer** in a food chain?

## Section B (Grade 6–7 Level)

**8.** What is a **cell**? What is the difference between a **plant cell** and an **animal cell**?

**9.** Name the levels of a food chain in order (producer → top predator). Give an example chain with 4 levels.

**10.** What is **heredity**? If both parents have brown eyes but their child has blue eyes, what might explain that?

**11.** Name three **organ systems** in the human body and what each one does.

**12.** What does a **microscope** do, and when would a scientist use one?

**13.** What is the difference between a **vertebrate** and an **invertebrate**? Give two examples of each.

**14.** What is the **scientific method**? List the steps in order.

## Section C (Grade 8 Level)

**15.** Describe what happens during **mitosis**. Why do cells divide?

**16.** What is **DNA**? Where is it found in a cell?

**17.** Complete this Punnett square: Cross two heterozygous parents (Bb × Bb). What percentage of offspring will show the dominant trait?

**18.** Explain the difference between **photosynthesis** and **cellular respiration**. How are they related?

**19.** What is **natural selection**? Give an example.

**20.** What is the difference between an **ecosystem** and a **biome**?

## Section D (Grade 9–10 Level)

**21.** Name the four types of **macromolecules** found in living things and give a function of each.

**22.** Describe the process of **protein synthesis** (transcription and translation) in basic terms.

**23.** What is the difference between **mitosis** and **meiosis**? When does each occur?

**24.** Explain the **lock and key model** of enzyme function. What happens if an enzyme is denatured?

**25.** Describe three types of **evidence for evolution**.

**26.** What is **carrying capacity** in an ecosystem? What happens to a population that exceeds it?

**27.** Compare **aerobic** and **anaerobic** respiration. Which produces more ATP?

**28.** A mutation occurs in a somatic cell vs. a gamete cell. Which can be passed to offspring? Why?
