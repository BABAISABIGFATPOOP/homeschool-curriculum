# Grade 6 (Age 11–12)

## Math
- Ratios, rates, & proportions
- Fractions, decimals, & percents (conversions)
- Integers & the number line (positive/negative)
- Intro to algebraic expressions & equations
- Area, surface area, & volume
- Basic statistics: mean, median, mode, range
- Coordinate graphing

## English
- Reading: longer novels, nonfiction articles, mythology
- Grammar: clauses, phrases, complex sentences, comma rules
- Writing: research reports, narrative essays, journal writing
- Vocabulary: Latin/Greek roots
- Intro to MLA citation & note-taking

## Biology
- Cell biology deeper dive: organelles, cell division basics
- Body systems: circulatory & nervous systems
- Classification of organisms (kingdoms of life)
- Intro to genetics: traits, heredity basics
- Ecology: biomes, adaptations, biodiversity
- Lab skills: microscope use, recording observations
