# Grade 6 (Age 11–12)

## Math
- Ratios, rates, & proportions
- Fractions, decimals, & percents (conversions)
- Integers & the number line (positive/negative)
- Intro to algebraic expressions & equations
- Area, surface area, & volume
- Basic statistics: mean, median, mode, range
- Coordinate graphing

## English
- Reading: longer novels, nonfiction articles, mythology
- Grammar: clauses, phrases, complex sentences, comma rules
- Writing: research reports, narrative essays, journal writing
- Vocabulary: Latin/Greek roots
- Intro to MLA citation & note-taking

## Biology
- Cell biology deeper dive: organelles, cell division basics
- Body systems: circulatory & nervous systems
- Classification of organisms (kingdoms of life)
- Intro to genetics: traits, heredity basics
- Ecology: biomes, adaptations, biodiversity
- Lab skills: microscope use, recording observations

## Science
- Energy (kinetic, potential, thermal; energy transfers & transformations)
- Intro to chemistry (atoms, elements, compounds, mixtures)
- Earth's layers (crust, mantle, core; structure & composition)
- Plate tectonics (continental drift, plate boundaries, earthquakes, volcanoes)
- Weather systems (air masses, fronts, severe weather, forecasting)

## Spanish
- Present tense: all regular verbs (-AR, -ER, -IR conjugations)
- Ser vs. estar (to be — permanent vs. temporary)
- Adjective agreement (gender & number)
- Telling time (¿Qué hora es?)
- Describing people & places (physical & personality traits)
- Basic conversation skills & sentence building

## Chinese (Mandarin)
- Present, past, and future with 了 (completed), 会 (will), 要 (going to)
- Measure words: 个, 只, 本, 条, 张, 杯, 块
- Asking questions with 吗, 什么, 哪里, 谁, 怎么, 几, 多少
- Description words: adjectives with 很, comparisons intro
- Time expressions: 今天, 昨天, 明天, 现在, 以前, 以后
- Reading and writing short dialogues
- ~200 characters by end of year

## Geography
- World regions overview (Africa, Asia, Europe, Americas, Oceania)
- Physical vs political maps
- Population & settlement patterns

## History
- Ancient civilizations (Mesopotamia, Egypt, Greece, Rome, China, India)
- Rise of empires
- Trade routes (Silk Road)
- World religions origins
- Culture & geography connection
