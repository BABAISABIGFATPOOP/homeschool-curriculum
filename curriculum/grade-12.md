# Grade 12 (Age 17–18)

## Math (Calculus / AP Calculus AB)
- Limits & continuity
- Derivatives (definition, rules, chain rule, implicit differentiation)
- Applications of derivatives (optimization, related rates, curve sketching)
- Integrals (antiderivatives, definite integrals, FTC)
- Applications of integrals (area, volume, accumulation)
- Differential equations intro

## English (British & World Literature)
- Beowulf & medieval literature
- Shakespeare deep dive (Hamlet, Macbeth, or King Lear)
- Romantic poets (Keats, Shelley, Byron, Wordsworth)
- Victorian literature (Dickens, Bronte, Wilde)
- Modern & postmodern world literature (Kafka, Orwell, Achebe, Marquez)
- Senior thesis / capstone research paper
- College-level writing

## Biology (AP Biology / Environmental Science)
- Advanced molecular biology & genetics
- Biotechnology & bioinformatics
- Ecology & environmental science in depth
- Conservation biology & sustainability
- Capstone research project
- AP exam preparation (if AP track)

## Science (AP Physics or AP Chemistry)
- AP Physics track: mechanics in depth, electricity & magnetism, waves, modern physics
- AP Chemistry track: thermodynamics, equilibrium, kinetics, electrochemistry, nuclear chemistry, organic chemistry intro

## Spanish
- Near-fluency conversation & debate
- Reading full novels/plays in Spanish
- AP Spanish Language prep
- Cultural research projects

## Chinese (Mandarin)
- Advanced reading & writing
- Literary analysis in Chinese
- Academic & professional Chinese
- HSK 5-6 level preparation
- ~1500+ characters

## Geography
- Geopolitics & international relations
- Environmental geography & climate policy
- Urban planning & development
- GIS applications
- Capstone geography project

## History (Government & Economics / AP)
- US Government & Politics (Constitution, branches, civil liberties, elections, policy)
- Economics (micro & macro: supply/demand, markets, fiscal/monetary policy, international trade)
- AP exam preparation
