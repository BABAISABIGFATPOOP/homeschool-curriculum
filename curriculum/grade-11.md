# Grade 11 (Age 16–17)

## Math (Pre-Calculus)
- Functions review (polynomial, rational, exponential, logarithmic)
- Trigonometry (unit circle, trig functions, identities, equations)
- Sequences & series (arithmetic, geometric, summation notation)
- Conic sections (parabolas, ellipses, hyperbolas, circles)
- Polar coordinates & parametric equations
- Limits intro (preparing for calculus)

## English (American Literature)
- Colonial & revolutionary literature (Bradstreet, Franklin, Paine)
- Romanticism & Transcendentalism (Emerson, Thoreau, Whitman, Dickinson)
- Realism & Naturalism (Twain, Crane, Chopin)
- Harlem Renaissance & Modernism (Hughes, Fitzgerald, Hemingway)
- Contemporary American literature
- Research paper, literary analysis, college application essays
- SAT/ACT prep integrated

## Biology (AP Biology or Anatomy & Physiology)
- Advanced cell biology & biochemistry
- Molecular genetics & biotechnology
- Evolution & population genetics (Hardy-Weinberg)
- Comparative anatomy & physiology (all systems in depth)
- Ecology & environmental science
- Independent research & lab work

## Science (Physics)
- Kinematics (1D and 2D motion, projectiles)
- Newton's laws & dynamics (forces, friction, circular motion)
- Work, energy, power & conservation laws
- Momentum & collisions
- Waves & sound
- Electricity & magnetism intro
- Optics

## Spanish (if included — advanced)
- Subjunctive in all tenses
- Literary analysis in Spanish
- Essay writing (argumentative, analytical)
- Advanced conversation & debate
- Cultural deep dives (literature, film, current events)

## Chinese (Mandarin) (if included — advanced)
- Advanced grammar & sentence patterns
- Reading authentic texts (news, literature, essays)
- Formal & academic writing
- Classical Chinese continued
- HSK 4-5 level preparation
- ~1200+ characters

## Geography
- AP Human Geography topics: population, migration, cultural patterns, political geography, agriculture, urbanization, industrialization

## History (US History / AP US History)
- Colonial America through present day (comprehensive)
- Document-based questions (DBQs)
- Thematic essays
- Primary source analysis
- Preparing for AP exam format
