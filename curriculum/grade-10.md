# Grade 10 (Age 15–16)

## Math (Geometry)
- Points, lines, planes, and angles
- Proofs: two-column, paragraph, flowchart
- Triangles: congruence, similarity, trigonometric ratios (SOH-CAH-TOA)
- Quadrilaterals & polygons
- Circles: arcs, chords, tangents, inscribed angles
- Area & volume of 3D solids
- Coordinate geometry proofs
- Transformations: translations, rotations, reflections, dilations

## English
- Reading: world literature, American literature survey, poetry analysis
- Grammar: style refinement, tone, voice
- Writing: advanced research papers, literary analysis, college-prep essays
- Vocabulary: continued SAT/ACT prep
- Rhetoric & argumentation: evaluating sources, synthesis essays
- Oral presentations & seminar discussions

## Biology (or AP Biology Prep)
- Review & deepen: molecular biology, genetics, evolution
- Anatomy & physiology: organ systems in depth
- Microbiology: viruses, bacteria, disease & immunity
- Plant biology: structure, reproduction, transpiration
- Bioethics: genetic engineering, cloning, environmental policy
- Ecology: biosphere-level thinking, sustainability
- Independent research project or science fair
