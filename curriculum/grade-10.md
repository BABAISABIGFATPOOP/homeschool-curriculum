# Grade 10 (Age 15–16)

## Math (Geometry)
- Points, lines, planes, and angles
- Proofs: two-column, paragraph, flowchart
- Triangles: congruence, similarity, trigonometric ratios (SOH-CAH-TOA)
- Quadrilaterals & polygons
- Circles: arcs, chords, tangents, inscribed angles
- Area & volume of 3D solids
- Coordinate geometry proofs
- Transformations: translations, rotations, reflections, dilations

## English
- Reading: world literature, American literature survey, poetry analysis
- Grammar: style refinement, tone, voice
- Writing: advanced research papers, literary analysis, college-prep essays
- Vocabulary: continued SAT/ACT prep
- Rhetoric & argumentation: evaluating sources, synthesis essays
- Oral presentations & seminar discussions

## Biology (or AP Biology Prep)
- Review & deepen: molecular biology, genetics, evolution
- Anatomy & physiology: organ systems in depth
- Microbiology: viruses, bacteria, disease & immunity
- Plant biology: structure, reproduction, transpiration
- Bioethics: genetic engineering, cloning, environmental policy
- Ecology: biosphere-level thinking, sustainability
- Independent research project or science fair

## Science (Chemistry)
- Atomic theory (historical models, modern atomic model, subatomic particles)
- Electron configuration (orbitals, energy levels, electron filling order, noble gas notation)
- Chemical bonding: ionic (electron transfer, lattice structures, properties of ionic compounds)
- Chemical bonding: covalent (electron sharing, Lewis structures, molecular geometry intro, polarity)
- Stoichiometry (mole concept, molar mass, mole ratios, limiting reagents, percent yield)
- States of matter & gas laws (kinetic molecular theory, Boyle's, Charles's, ideal gas law)
- Acids & bases (pH scale, indicators, neutralization reactions, strong vs. weak)
- Reaction types (synthesis, decomposition, single replacement, double replacement, combustion)

## Spanish
- Advanced subjunctive (imperfect subjunctive, si clauses)
- Perfect tenses (present perfect, past perfect, future perfect)
- Passive voice (ser + past participle, se constructions)
- Literary analysis in Spanish (theme, character, symbolism)
- Essay writing in Spanish (argumentative & analytical)
- Cultural studies: Latin America & Spain (history, art, traditions, current events)

## Chinese (Mandarin)
- Classical Chinese (文言文) introduction: basic particles and sentence structures
- Formal essay writing: opinion essays, descriptive essays
- Literary excerpts: reading simplified versions of classic Chinese texts
- Advanced grammar patterns: 越来越, 一边...一边..., 除了...以外, 连...都/也
- Cultural studies: festivals, history, philosophy, arts
- ~1000 characters by end of year

## Geography
- AP-level topics (geopolitics, development & inequality, agriculture & food systems, industrialization, environmental sustainability, GIS & spatial analysis)

## History
- AP-level topics: political revolutions, nationalism & imperialism
- Global conflicts
- Post-WWII world order
- Globalization
- Human rights
- Contemporary issues
- Historiography
