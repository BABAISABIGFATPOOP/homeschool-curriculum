# Grade 4 (Age 9–10)

## Math
- Place value through hundred thousands
- Multi-digit addition & subtraction
- Intro to multiplication & division (up to 12×12)
- Fractions: reading, comparing, simple equivalents
- Basic geometry: shapes, perimeter, area
- Intro to measurement & unit conversion
- Simple word problems & patterns

## English
- Reading: chapter books, fiction & nonfiction comprehension
- Grammar: parts of speech, sentence types, subject-verb agreement
- Writing: paragraphs, personal narratives, book reports
- Spelling & vocabulary building
- Intro to cursive/handwriting refinement

## Biology
- Living vs. nonliving things
- Plant life cycles & parts of a plant
- Animal classifications (mammals, reptiles, birds, fish, amphibians, insects)
- Habitats & ecosystems intro
- Human body basics: skeletal & muscular systems
- Observation & simple experiments

## Science
- Weather & climate basics (temperature, precipitation, seasons)
- States of matter (solid, liquid, gas) and changes between them
- Magnets (poles, attract/repel, magnetic fields)
- Simple machines (lever, pulley, inclined plane, wheel & axle, wedge, screw)
- Rocks & minerals (types of rocks, identification, properties)

## Spanish
- Greetings & introductions (hola, adiós, me llamo...)
- Numbers 1–100
- Colors
- Family members (mamá, papá, hermano, hermana, etc.)
- Basic phrases (por favor, gracias, ¿cómo estás?)
- Classroom objects (libro, lápiz, mesa, silla, etc.)

## Chinese (Mandarin)
- Pinyin system: initials, finals, and tone marks
- Four tones: 1st (flat), 2nd (rising), 3rd (dipping), 4th (falling)
- Greetings and polite phrases: 你好, 谢谢, 再见, 对不起, 没关系
- Numbers 1–100 (一 through 一百)
- Family members: 爸爸, 妈妈, 哥哥, 姐姐, 弟弟, 妹妹, 爷爷, 奶奶
- Colors: 红, 蓝, 绿, 黄, 白, 黑, 橙, 紫
- Basic strokes and stroke order principles
- Character writing: first simple characters (大, 小, 人, 口, 日, 月)
