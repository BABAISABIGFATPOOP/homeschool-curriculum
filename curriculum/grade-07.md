# Grade 7 (Age 12–13)

## Math
- Proportional relationships & percentages
- Operations with rational numbers
- Two-step equations & inequalities
- Geometry: angle relationships, circles, scale drawings
- Probability & statistics
- Intro to functions
- Real-world math applications

## English
- Reading: classic & contemporary fiction, memoir, drama
- Grammar: active/passive voice, misplaced modifiers, semicolons
- Writing: argumentative essays, literary analysis, creative writing
- Vocabulary expansion & SAT-prep word lists begin
- Public speaking & debate intro

## Biology
- Human body systems: endocrine, immune, reproductive (age-appropriate)
- DNA & genetics: Punnett squares, dominant/recessive traits
- Evolution & natural selection intro
- Ecology: populations, communities, human impact
- Nutrition & health
- Designing controlled experiments

## Science
- Newton's laws of motion (inertia, F=ma, action-reaction)
- Chemical reactions basics (reactants, products, conservation of mass, signs of a reaction)
- Periodic table intro (organization, groups, periods, metals/nonmetals/metalloids)
- Earth's atmosphere (layers, composition, air pressure, wind)
- Space & astronomy (stars, galaxies, life cycle of stars, Big Bang intro, telescopes)

## Spanish
- Irregular present tense verbs (ir, tener, querer, poder, hacer, decir)
- Preterite tense introduction (regular verbs)
- Reflexive verbs (levantarse, ducharse, acostarse)
- Por vs. para
- House & city vocabulary (rooms, furniture, places in town)
- Giving & understanding directions

## Chinese (Mandarin)
- Comparison with 比 (A 比 B + adjective)
- Giving directions: 左, 右, 前, 后, 东, 南, 西, 北
- Shopping and money: 多少钱, 块/元, 太贵了, 便宜
- Hobbies and interests: 喜欢, 爱好, sports, music, reading vocabulary
- Time expressions: clock time, duration with 了, 从...到...
- Reading short passages and answering comprehension questions
- ~350 characters by end of year

## Geography
- Africa & Asia in depth (physical features, climate, resources, cultures, current issues)
- Migration patterns
- Urbanization

## History
- Medieval world (feudalism, Byzantine Empire, Islamic Golden Age, African kingdoms, Mongol Empire)
- Renaissance
- Reformation
- Age of Exploration
