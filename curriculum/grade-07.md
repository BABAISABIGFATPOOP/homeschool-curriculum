# Grade 7 (Age 12–13)

## Math
- Proportional relationships & percentages
- Operations with rational numbers
- Two-step equations & inequalities
- Geometry: angle relationships, circles, scale drawings
- Probability & statistics
- Intro to functions
- Real-world math applications

## English
- Reading: classic & contemporary fiction, memoir, drama
- Grammar: active/passive voice, misplaced modifiers, semicolons
- Writing: argumentative essays, literary analysis, creative writing
- Vocabulary expansion & SAT-prep word lists begin
- Public speaking & debate intro

## Biology
- Human body systems: endocrine, immune, reproductive (age-appropriate)
- DNA & genetics: Punnett squares, dominant/recessive traits
- Evolution & natural selection intro
- Ecology: populations, communities, human impact
- Nutrition & health
- Designing controlled experiments
