# Grade 9 (Age 14–15)

## Math (Algebra I)
- Solving linear equations & inequalities
- Systems of equations (graphing, substitution, elimination)
- Polynomials: operations & factoring
- Quadratic equations & the quadratic formula
- Exponent rules & radical expressions
- Functions: domain, range, graphing
- Word problems & modeling

## English
- Reading: classic literature (To Kill a Mockingbird, Romeo & Juliet, etc.)
- Grammar: polishing for formal writing
- Writing: analytical essays, thesis statements, research papers
- Vocabulary: SAT/ACT prep integrated
- Rhetoric: persuasion, logical fallacies, argument structure
- Intro to literary criticism

## Biology (High School Biology)
- Biochemistry: macromolecules, enzymes, water properties
- Cell structure & function (detailed)
- Cellular energy: photosynthesis & cellular respiration (chemical equations)
- DNA, RNA, protein synthesis
- Genetics: Mendelian & non-Mendelian, biotechnology intro
- Evolution: evidence, mechanisms, speciation
- Ecology: ecosystems, population dynamics, human impact
- Lab work throughout (this is a lab science course)

## Science (Physical Science)
- Kinematics (distance, displacement, speed, velocity, acceleration, graphing motion)
- Forces & Newton's laws detailed (free-body diagrams, net force, friction, weight vs. mass)
- Work, energy, & power (calculating work, kinetic/potential energy, conservation of energy, power)
- Intro to chemistry: atomic structure (protons, neutrons, electrons, atomic number, mass number, isotopes)
- Intro to chemistry: periodic trends (electronegativity, ionization energy, atomic radius)
- Intro to chemistry: chemical equations (balancing equations, types of reactions, stoichiometry basics)

## Spanish
- Subjunctive mood (expressions of doubt, emotion, desire, recommendation)
- Conditional tense (yo haría, tú dirías)
- Future tense (hablaré, comeré, viviré)
- Relative pronouns (que, quien, lo que, el cual)
- Formal writing in Spanish (letters, structured paragraphs)
- Reading literature excerpts in Spanish
- Debate & discussion in Spanish

## Chinese (Mandarin)
- 把 construction (把 + object + verb + complement)
- Passive voice with 被 (被字句)
- Chengyu (成语) introduction: common four-character idioms and their stories
- Writing structured paragraphs on various topics
- Reading simplified news articles and blog posts
- Expanding vocabulary for school, travel, and daily life topics
- ~700 characters by end of year
