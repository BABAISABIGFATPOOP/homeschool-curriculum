# Grade 5 (Age 10–11)

## Math
- Place value through millions
- Long multiplication & division
- Fractions: add, subtract, multiply; mixed numbers
- Decimals: place value, operations
- Volume & area of rectangles/triangles
- Coordinate plane intro
- Order of operations

## English
- Reading: novels, poetry, short stories; identifying themes
- Grammar: verb tenses, pronouns, conjunctions, punctuation
- Writing: five-paragraph essays, persuasive writing, summaries
- Vocabulary: context clues, prefixes/suffixes
- Oral presentations & book discussions

## Biology
- Cells: basic structure (plant vs. animal)
- Microorganisms intro (bacteria, fungi)
- Food chains & food webs
- Photosynthesis (simple overview)
- Human body: digestive & respiratory systems
- Scientific method introduction
