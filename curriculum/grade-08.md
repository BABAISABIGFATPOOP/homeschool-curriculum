# Grade 8 (Age 13–14)

## Math
- Linear equations & systems (intro)
- Slope & graphing lines
- Exponents & scientific notation
- Pythagorean theorem
- Transformations & congruence
- Functions & function notation
- Intro to irrational numbers
- Scatter plots & data analysis

## English
- Reading: novels, Shakespeare intro, informational texts
- Grammar: sentence variety, parallel structure, advanced punctuation
- Writing: research papers (with citations), comparative essays, poetry
- Vocabulary: SAT-level words, discipline-specific terms
- Critical thinking & media literacy

## Biology
- Cellular processes: mitosis, meiosis, photosynthesis, respiration
- Genetics: genotype/phenotype, mutations, genetic disorders
- Taxonomy & dichotomous keys
- Human anatomy review & integration of systems
- Ecology: energy flow, nutrient cycles, conservation
- Lab: dissection intro, controlled experiments
