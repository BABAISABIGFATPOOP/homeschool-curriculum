# Analyzing and Interpreting Literature CLEP Practice Exam 2

**Time Limit:** 90 minutes
**Number of Questions:** 80
**Format:** Multiple choice (A, B, C, D)
**Passing Score:** Typically 50 (on CLEP's 20–80 scale); check with your institution.

Read the following poem and answer questions 1–6.

> Two roads diverged in a yellow wood,
> And sorry I could not travel both

>
