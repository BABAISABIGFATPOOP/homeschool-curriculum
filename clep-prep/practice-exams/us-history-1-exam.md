- **1.** Jamestown (1607) was the first permanent English settlement. Roanoke (1587) was the "Lost Colony." St. Augustine was Spanish.
- **10.** Bacon's Rebellion revealed class tensions; afterward, Virginia planters shifted more heavily to enslaved African labor.
- **44.** The Great Compromise (Connecticut Compromise) created the House (proportional) and Senate (equal).
- **57.** Marbury v. Madison (1803) is the landmark case establishing judicial review.
- **86.** Dred Scott was a deeply controversial decision that inflamed sectional tensions.
- **96.** The Emancipation Proclamation was a wartime measure that only applied to states in rebellion, not border states.
- **115.** The Compromise of 1877 resolved the disputed 1876 election and ended federal Reconstruction.
- **119.** Approximately 620,000 soldiers died in the Civil War (more recent scholarship suggests even higher).
