# CLEP United States History II (1865 to Present) — Practice Exam 3

**Time Limit:** 90 minutes
**Number of Questions:** 120
**Format:** Multiple choice (A, B, C, D)
**Passing Score:** Typically 50 (on CLEP's 20–80 scale); check with your institution.
**Coverage:** Reconstruction through the modern era
