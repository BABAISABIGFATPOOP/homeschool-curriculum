# CLEP College Mathematics — Full Practice Exam

**Time Limit:** 90 minutes
**Number of Questions:** 60
**Format:** Multiple choice (A, B, C, D)
**Passing Score:** Typically 50 (on CLEP's 20–80 scale); approximately 50% correct is a reasonable target, but check with your institution.
**Calculator:** A scientific (nongraphing) calculator is provided on-screen during the real exam.

### Answer Explanations (Selected)

- **1.** A ∩ B contains elements common to both sets: {3, 4, 5}.
- **5.** A conditional with a false hypothesis is always true (vacuous truth).
- **6.** 60 + 50 − 20 = 90 study at least one; 100 − 90 = 10 study neither.
- **10.** g(3) = 2(3)+1 = 7; f(7) = 49.
- **12.** C(3,2) × (1/2)³ = 3/8.
- **23.** Q1 = 4, Q3 = 12; IQR = 12 − 4 = 8.
- **26.** √((4−1)² + (6−2)²) = √(9+16) = 5.
- **32.** 1000(1.05)² = 1000(1.1025) = 1102.50.
- **34.** (1 + 0.12/12)¹² − 1 ≈ 0.1268 = 12.68%.
