# College Algebra CLEP Practice Exam 3

**Time Limit:** 90 minutes
**Number of Questions:** 60
**Format:** Multiple choice (A, B, C, D)
**Passing Score:** Typically 50 (on CLEP's 20–80 scale); check with your institution.
**Calculator:** A graphing calculator is NOT permitted on the real exam. A scientific (nongraphing) calculator is provided on-screen.

---
