# CLEP Test Prep

## What are CLEP Tests?

CLEP (College-Level Examination Program) tests let you earn **college credit** by passing an exam — no college class required. There are 34 CLEP exams covering subjects you may already know from your homeschool curriculum.

- Each exam costs **$93** (as of 2025)
- Most exams are **90 minutes**, multiple choice
- A passing score earns you **3–12 college credits** depending on the exam
- Over **2,900 colleges** accept CLEP credits
- You can take them at any age — many homeschoolers take them in high school

## How to Use This Section

1. Pick an exam that matches what you've been studying
2. Review the study guide — it covers every topic on the exam
3. Use the practice questions to test yourself
4. When you feel ready, register at [clep.collegeboard.org](https://clep.collegeboard.org)
5. Take the exam at an authorized testing center

## Recommended CLEP Exams by Subject

### Math
- **College Mathematics** — Covers algebra, geometry, statistics, probability, logic
- **College Algebra** — Covers equations, inequalities, functions, graphing, polynomials

### English
- **College Composition** — Essay writing, revision, grammar, rhetorical analysis
- **Analyzing & Interpreting Literature** — Poetry, prose, drama analysis

### Biology
- **Biology** — Molecular/cellular biology, genetics, evolution, ecology, organisms

### Science
- **Chemistry** — Atomic theory, bonding, reactions, stoichiometry, states of matter, acids/bases
- **Natural Sciences** — Biological + physical science overview

### History
- **History of the United States I** — Colonization through 1877
- **History of the United States II** — 1865 to present
- **Western Civilization I** — Ancient Near East through 1648
- **Western Civilization II** — 1648 to present

### Spanish
- **Spanish Language (Levels 1 & 2)** — Reading, grammar, listening comprehension

## Tips for Success

- Start with subjects you've studied most — you may already know enough to pass
- Focus on **weak areas** identified in practice questions
- Use the study guides here + free online resources
- Take a practice exam under timed conditions before the real thing
- You can retake a CLEP exam after **3 months** if you don't pass
