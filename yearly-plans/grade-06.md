# Grade 6 — Full Year Plan (36 Weeks)

## Math

### Quarter 1 (Weeks 1–9)
| Week | Topic | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| 1 | Whole Number Operations Review | Multi-digit multiplication & division fluency |
| 2 | Factors & Multiples | GCF, LCM, prime factorization |
| 3 | Intro to Ratios | What ratios are, writing ratios, equivalent ratios |
| 4 | Rates & Unit Rates | Speed, price per unit, comparing rates |
| 5 | Ratio Tables & Tape Diagrams | Solving ratio problems with visual models |
| 6 | Fractions Review | Operations with fractions, mixed numbers |
| 7 | Dividing Fractions | Fraction ÷ fraction, reciprocals |
| 8 | Fraction & Ratio Word Problems | Multi-step real-world problems |
| 9 | Review & Assessment | Quarter 1 test |

### Quarter 2 (Weeks 10–18)
| Week | Topic | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| 10 | Decimals & Place Value Review | Through thousandths, all operations |
| 11 | Percents Intro | What percent means, converting fractions/decimals/percents |
| 12 | Percent Problems | Finding percent of a number, discounts, tax, tips |
| 13 | Integers Intro | Positive & negative numbers, number line |
| 14 | Comparing & Ordering Integers | Absolute value, real-world contexts (temperature, debt) |
| 15 | Adding & Subtracting Integers | Rules & patterns, number line model |
| 16 | Multiplying & Dividing Integers | Rules for signs |
| 17 | Integer Word Problems | Real-world applications |
| 18 | Review & Assessment | Quarter 2 test |

### Quarter 3 (Weeks 19–27)
| Week | Topic | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| 19 | Algebraic Expressions | Variables, writing expressions from words |
| 20 | Evaluating Expressions | Substitution, order of operations with variables |
| 21 | Equivalent Expressions | Combining like terms, distributive property |
| 22 | One-Step Equations | Solving using inverse operations (addition/subtraction) |
| 23 | One-Step Equations | Solving using inverse operations (multiplication/division) |
| 24 | One-Step Inequalities | Solving & graphing on a number line |
| 25 | Coordinate Plane | All four quadrants, plotting & reading points |
| 26 | Graphing Relationships | Tables to graphs, identifying patterns |
| 27 | Review & Assessment | Quarter 3 test |

### Quarter 4 (Weeks 28–36)
| Week | Topic | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| 28 | Area: Parallelograms & Triangles | Formulas, calculating, word problems |
| 29 | Area: Trapezoids & Composite Shapes | Breaking shapes into simpler parts |
| 30 | Surface Area | Nets, surface area of rectangular prisms |
| 31 | Volume | Volume of rectangular prisms, composite prisms |
| 32 | Statistics: Mean, Median, Mode, Range | Calculating & comparing, choosing the best measure |
| 33 | Statistics: Data Displays | Dot plots, histograms, box plots |
| 34 | Statistics: Describing Distributions | Shape, center, spread, outliers |
| 35 | Review | Full year review |
| 36 | Final Assessment | End-of-year test |

## Biology

### Quarter 1 (Weeks 1–9)
| Week | Topic | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| 1 | Review: Cells | Cell theory, plant vs. animal cells |
| 2 | Cell Organelles | Nucleus, mitochondria, ER, Golgi, ribosomes, vacuole |
| 3 | Cell Organelles | Chloroplasts, cell wall, cell membrane — functions |
| 4 | Cell Membrane & Transport | How things move in/out of cells (diffusion, osmosis — simple) |
| 5 | Cell Division Intro | Why cells divide, growth & repair |
| 6 | Mitosis Overview | Stages of mitosis (simple), before & after |
| 7 | Microscope Skills | Parts of a microscope, preparing slides, observing cells |
| 8 | Lab: Observing Cells | Onion skin, cheek cells, drawing what you see |
| 9 | Review & Assessment | Quarter 1 test, cell model project |

### Quarter 2 (Weeks 10–18)
| Week | Topic | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| 10 | Human Body: Circulatory System | Heart, blood vessels, blood, circulation path |
| 11 | Human Body: Circulatory System Lab | Heart rate experiments, diagram labeling |
| 12 | Human Body: Nervous System | Brain, spinal cord, nerves, reflexes |
| 13 | Human Body: Nervous System Lab | Reaction time experiments |
| 14 | Human Body: Immune System Intro | White blood cells, fighting infection, fever |
| 15 | Disease & Prevention | Bacteria vs. viruses, how diseases spread, hygiene |
| 16 | Classification: Domains & Kingdoms | Three domains, six kingdoms overview |
| 17 | Classification: Using Dichotomous Keys | How to identify organisms step by step |
| 18 | Review & Assessment | Quarter 2 test |

### Quarter 3 (Weeks 19–27)
| Week | Topic | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| 19 | Genetics Intro | What is heredity, traits, genes |
| 20 | Dominant & Recessive Traits | Mendel's peas, how traits are passed down |
| 21 | Punnett Squares Intro | Single trait crosses, predicting offspring |
| 22 | Genetics Activities | Trait surveys (tongue rolling, earlobes), probability |
| 23 | DNA Intro | What DNA is, where it's found, double helix (simple) |
| 24 | Ecology: Biomes | Tropical, temperate, desert, tundra, aquatic |
| 25 | Ecology: Adaptations | How organisms adapt to their biome |
| 26 | Ecology: Biodiversity | Why diversity matters, threats to biodiversity |
| 27 | Review & Assessment | Quarter 3 test, genetics poster |

### Quarter 4 (Weeks 28–36)
| Week | Topic | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| 28 | Ecology: Populations & Communities | Population size, limiting factors, competition |

| 31 | Conservation & Sustainability | What we can do, case studies |
| 32 | Lab Skills Review | Designing experiments, variables, data collection |
| 33 | Independent Project | Choose a biology topic, research & plan |
| 34 | Independent Project | Create presentation (poster, slides, or report) |
| 35 | Review | Full year review |
| 36 | Final Assessment | End-of-year test, project presentations |
