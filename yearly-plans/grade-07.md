# Grade 7 — Full Year Plan (36 Weeks)

## Math

### Quarter 1 (Weeks 1–9)
| Week | Topic | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| 1 | Rational Numbers Review | Integers, fractions, decimals on the number line |
| 2 | Adding & Subtracting Rational Numbers | Positive/negative fractions & decimals |
| 3 | Multiplying & Dividing Rational Numbers | Sign rules, real-world contexts |
| 4 | Proportional Relationships | Identifying proportional relationships in tables & graphs |
| 5 | Unit Rates & Constants of Proportionality | Finding k in y = kx |
| 6 | Proportional Relationships in Equations | Writing & solving proportions |
| 7 | Percent Applications | Tax, tip, discount, markup, percent increase/decrease |
| 8 | Percent Applications | Simple interest, percent error |
| 9 | Review & Assessment | Quarter 1 test |

### Quarter 2 (Weeks 10–18)
| Week | Topic | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| 10 | Algebraic Expressions | Writing, simplifying, combining like terms |
| 11 | Distributive Property | Expanding expressions, factoring |
| 12 | Two-Step Equations | Solving ax + b = c |
| 13 | Two-Step Equations | Word problems, writing equations from contexts |
| 14 | Two-Step Inequalities | Solving & graphing, reversing inequality sign |
| 15 | Geometry: Angle Relationships | Complementary, supplementary, vertical, adjacent |
| 16 | Geometry: Triangles | Angle sum, exterior angles, triangle inequality |
| 17 | Geometry: Scale Drawings | Scale factor, creating & interpreting scale drawings |
| 18 | Review & Assessment | Quarter 2 test |

### Quarter 3 (Weeks 19–27)
| Week | Topic | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| 19 | Geometry: Circles | Circumference formula (C = πd), calculating |
| 20 | Geometry: Circles | Area formula (A = πr²), calculating |
| 21 | Geometry: Area of Composite Shapes | Breaking shapes apart, adding/subtracting areas |
| 22 | Geometry: Surface Area & Volume | Prisms & pyramids |
| 23 | Geometry: Cross-Sections | Slicing 3D shapes |
| 24 | Probability: Intro | Experimental vs. theoretical probability |
| 25 | Probability: Compound Events | Sample spaces, tree diagrams, counting principle |
| 26 | Probability: Simulations | Using simulations to estimate probability |
| 27 | Review & Assessment | Quarter 3 test |

### Quarter 4 (Weeks 28–36)
| Week | Topic | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| 28 | Statistics: Random Sampling | Why sampling matters, bias |
| 29 | Statistics: Comparing Populations | Using measures of center & variability |
| 30 | Statistics: Data Displays | Box plots, dot plots, histograms — comparing |
| 31 | Intro to Functions | Input/output, function tables, graphing |
| 32 | Linear vs. Nonlinear | Recognizing from tables & graphs |
| 33 | Real-World Math Projects | Budgeting, cooking (scaling recipes), travel planning |
| 34 | Problem Solving | Multi-step problems combining all topics |
| 35 | Review | Full year review |
| 36 | Final Assessment | End-of-year test |
