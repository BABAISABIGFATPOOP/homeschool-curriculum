# Grade 8 — Full Year Plan (36 Weeks)

## Math

### Quarter 1 (Weeks 1–9)
| Week | Topic | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| 1 | Number System Review | Rational vs. irrational numbers, classifying |
| 2 | Irrational Numbers | Approximating square roots, placing on number line |
| 3 | Exponents | Properties of exponents (product, quotient, power rules) |
| 4 | Negative & Zero Exponents | Patterns, simplifying |
| 5 | Scientific Notation | Converting to/from, comparing numbers |
| 6 | Operations with Scientific Notation | Adding, subtracting, multiplying, dividing |
| 7 | Linear Equations: One Step & Two Step | Review with rational numbers |
| 8 | Multi-Step Equations | Distributive property, combining like terms, variables on both sides |
| 9 | Review & Assessment | Quarter 1 test |

### Quarter 2 (Weeks 10–18)
| Week | Topic | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| 10 | Equations: Special Cases | No solution, infinite solutions |
| 11 | Proportional Relationships | y = mx, graphing, identifying from tables |
| 12 | Slope | Rise over run, from graphs, from two points, from equations |
| 13 | Slope-Intercept Form | y = mx + b, graphing lines, interpreting m and b |
| 14 | Writing Linear Equations | From graphs, tables, word problems |
| 15 | Systems of Equations Intro | Graphing method, finding intersection |
| 16 | Systems of Equations | Substitution method |
| 17 | Systems of Equations | Real-world applications |
| 18 | Review & Assessment | Quarter 2 test |

### Quarter 3 (Weeks 19–27)
| Week | Topic | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| 19 | Functions: Intro | What is a function, function notation, domain & range |
| 20 | Functions: Linear vs. Nonlinear | Comparing from tables, graphs, equations |
| 21 | Functions: Analyzing & Comparing | Rate of change, initial value |
| 22 | Functions: Qualitative Graphs | Sketching & interpreting real-world graphs |
| 23 | Transformations: Translations & Reflections | On the coordinate plane |
| 24 | Transformations: Rotations & Dilations | On the coordinate plane |
| 25 | Congruence & Similarity | Using transformations to define |
| 26 | Pythagorean Theorem | Proving, finding missing sides |
| 27 | Review & Assessment | Quarter 3 test |

### Quarter 4 (Weeks 28–36)
| Week | Topic | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| 28 | Pythagorean Theorem Applications | Distance between two points, 3D problems |
| 29 | Volume: Cylinders | Formula, calculating, word problems |
| 30 | Volume: Cones & Spheres | Formulas, calculating, comparing |
| 31 | Scatter Plots | Plotting data, identifying patterns |
| 32 | Line of Best Fit | Drawing, using to make predictions |
| 33 | Two-Way Tables | Organizing categorical data, relative frequency |
| 34 | Problem Solving | Multi-topic challenge problems |
| 35 | Review | Full year review, algebra readiness |
| 36 | Final Assessment | End-of-year test |

## Biology

### Quarter 1 (Weeks 1–9)
| Week | Topic | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| 1 | Review: Cells | Cell theory, organelles, plant vs. animal |
| 2 | Cell Membrane in Detail | Phospholipid bilayer, selective permeability |
| 3 | Cell Transport | Diffusion, osmosis, active transport |
| 4 | Lab: Osmosis Experiment | Egg in vinegar/corn syrup, observing osmosis |
| 5 | Photosynthesis | Light reactions overview, equation: 6CO₂ + 6H₂O → C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6O₂ |
| 6 | Photosynthesis Lab | Testing variables (light intensity, color) on plant growth |
| 7 | Cellular Respiration | Equation: C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6O₂ → 6CO₂ + 6H₂O + ATP |
| 8 | Photosynthesis vs. Respiration | Comparing, how they're connected, energy flow |
| 9 | Review & Assessment | Quarter 1 test |

### Quarter 2 (Weeks 10–18)
| Week | Topic | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| 10 | Mitosis Review | Stages: prophase, metaphase, anaphase, telophase |
| 11 | Mitosis Lab | Observing onion root tip cells under microscope |
| 12 | Meiosis | Stages, how it differs from mitosis |
| 13 | Meiosis | Crossing over, genetic variation, gametes |
| 14 | Comparing Mitosis & Meiosis | Side-by-side comparison, when each occurs |
| 15 | Genetics: Genotype & Phenotype | Homozygous, heterozygous, alleles |
| 16 | Punnett Squares: Advanced | Incomplete dominance, codominance, sex-linked traits |
| 17 | Mutations | Gene mutations, chromosomal mutations, causes & effects |
| 18 | Review & Assessment | Quarter 2 test, genetics problem set |

### Quarter 3 (Weeks 19–27)
| Week | Topic | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| 19 | Taxonomy | Linnaean classification, binomial nomenclature |
| 20 | Dichotomous Keys | Using and creating keys to classify organisms |
| 21 | Domains & Kingdoms | Bacteria, Archaea, Eukarya; characteristics of each kingdom |
| 22 | Human Anatomy: Systems Integration | How circulatory, respiratory, digestive work together |
| 23 | Human Anatomy: Nervous & Endocrine | How body systems communicate |
| 24 | Human Anatomy: Musculoskeletal | Bones, muscles, joints — how we move |
| 25 | Dissection Intro | Safety, tools, external anatomy (earthworm or similar) |
| 26 | Dissection Lab | Internal anatomy, identifying organs & systems |
| 27 | Review & Assessment | Quarter 3 test |

### Quarter 4 (Weeks 28–36)
| Week | Topic | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| 28 | Ecology: Energy Flow | Trophic levels, energy pyramids, 10% rule |
| 29 | Ecology: Nutrient Cycles | Carbon cycle, nitrogen cycle, water cycle |
| 30 | Ecology: Succession | Primary & secondary succession |
| 31 | Ecology: Conservation | Biodiversity, endangered species, human responsibility |
| 32 | Lab: Ecosystem in a Bottle | Build a mini ecosystem, observe over time |
| 33 | Independent Research Project | Choose a biology topic, research |
| 34 | Independent Research Project | Create presentation or paper |
| 35 | Review | Full year review |
| 36 | Final Assessment | End-of-year test, project presentations |
